The Best Movies According to Hillary

My top 5 movies of all-time.

Beauty and the Beast

"Beauty and the Beast" is a Disney animated film that tells the story of Belle, a young woman who trades her freedom to save her father from a cursed prince turned into a beast. Over time, Belle and the Beast form a deep bond, and Belle's kindness and bravery help to break the curse. The film highlights themes of love, redemption, and looking beyond appearances. It is celebrated for its memorable characters, beautiful animation, and enchanting musical score.

Coco

"Coco" is a Disney-Pixar animated film that follows the story of Miguel, a young boy who dreams of becoming a musician despite his family's ban on music. On the Day of the Dead, Miguel is accidentally transported to the Land of the Dead, where he seeks the help of his great-great-grandfather, a legendary singer, to return to the living world and uncover his family's past. The film explores themes of family, cultural heritage, and the importance of remembering loved ones. "Coco" is acclaimed for its vibrant animation, heartfelt storytelling, and rich depiction of Mexican traditions.

Ratatouille

"Ratatouille" is a Disney-Pixar animated film that tells the story of Remy, a rat with a passion for cooking, who dreams of becoming a great chef in Paris. Despite the obvious challenges, Remy forms an unlikely partnership with Linguini, a young kitchen worker, and together they create culinary masterpieces that impress even the harshest critic. The film celebrates following one's dreams and talents, regardless of obstacles or stereotypes. "Ratatouille" is praised for its charming characters, delightful animation, and its inspiring message about pursuing one's passions.

Princess and the Frog

"The Princess and the Frog" is a Disney animated film set in New Orleans that follows Tiana, a hardworking waitress who dreams of opening her own restaurant. Her life takes an unexpected turn when she kisses Prince Naveen, who has been turned into a frog by a voodoo curse, and she too becomes a frog. Together, they embark on a journey through the bayous to find a way to break the spell. The film is celebrated for its vibrant animation, catchy music, and its empowering message about determination and following one's dreams.

The Hunchback of Notre Dame

"The Hunchback of Notre Dame" is a Disney animated film that follows Quasimodo, the kind-hearted but deformed bell ringer of Notre Dame Cathedral, who longs to experience life outside the bell tower. He befriends the beautiful and compassionate gypsy Esmeralda and stands up against the tyrannical Judge Frollo, who seeks to destroy her and all who are different. Through their struggles, themes of acceptance, courage, and the battle against prejudice are explored. The film is noted for its dark, mature themes, powerful musical score, and rich, detailed animation.
